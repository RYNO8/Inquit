About

Inquit is a free messaging service

The client-side of Inquit was made possible with:

  • tkinter (GUI interface)
  • urllib (requests and bypassing school proxy)
  • zroya (Notifications)

The server-side of Inquit was made possible with:

  • Amazon Lambda
  • Amazon DynamoDB
